## Conclusions

- The Workshop's popularity is dominated by a handful of evergreen formats: auto-battlers, hero arenas/brawls, custom-hero/IMBA modes, tower defense, and Pudge/skillshot party modes.
- Progression hooks (save codes, ranked/MMR, talents/perks, shops) recur across the most-subscribed games — long-tail retention features, not just core gameplay.
- Custom Panorama UI (scoreboards, shops, talent trees, hero pickers) is a baseline expectation in top games — a strong argument for the MCP's Panorama tooling + docs.
